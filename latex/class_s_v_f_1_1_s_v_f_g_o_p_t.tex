\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Optimised \mbox{\hyperlink{class_s_v_f_1_1_s_v_f_g}{SVFG}}.
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Formal\+Param/\+Actual\+Ret is converted into Phi. Actual\+Param/\+Formal\+Ret becomes the operands of Phi nodes created at callee/caller\textquotesingle{}s entry/callsite.
\item Actual\+Ins/\+Actual\+Outs resides at direct call sites id removed. Sources of its incoming edges are connected with the destinations of its outgoing edges directly.
\item Formal\+Ins/\+Formal\+Outs reside at the entry/exit of non-\/address-\/taken functions is removed as Actual\+In/\+Actual\+Outs.
\item \mbox{\hyperlink{class_s_v_f_1_1_m_s_s_a_p_h_i}{MSSAPHI}} nodes are removed if it have no self cycle. Otherwise depends on user option. 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
